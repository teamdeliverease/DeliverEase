How It Works

Turning the quarantine into a quaranteam

Volunteers sign up to make deliveries

Volunteers rock! They’ll fulfill requests whenever they were originally planning on going to a store themselves, making it convenient to pick up requested items.

Anyone who could use help can submit a request below

Whether you have a particular vulnerability or are a healthcare practitioner intentionally staying away from public areas, we’re here to help.

We’ll pass requests to volunteers in your neighborhood

They’ll let us know when they can fulfill your request, and we’ll let you know when to expect your delivery.

Zero fees

No service or delivery fees! That means $0. Payments for groceries and purchased goods will be arranged between the volunteer and community members.

Submit a Request

DeliverEase will find you a volunteer in your neighborhood

You’ll get notified once we find a match for your request and a volunteer will drop off your items directly to your front door.

Thanks for submitting your request, we’ll do our best to fulfill it! We’ll reach out when a volunteer in your neighborhood can deliver your items.


You will be expected to pay for the items you ordered upon receipt of the delivery and coordinate with your volunteer on the methods of payment. If you have an online money transfer account (Paypal, Venmo, etc) please be prepared to share this information with your volunteer. If you can, try calling the store ahead and paying for your groceries over the phone. If you only have cash to pay for your groceries, please be prepared with exact change and put your cash in an envelope for your volunteer.

Reminders to volunteers:
  • Drop off deliveries at the doorstep to avoid coming in contact with the community member. If there are special delivery instructions (i.e. gate access), you will receive instructions from the DeliverEase team.
  • Maintain at least 6 feet distance between others while you grocery shop.
  • Avoid touching your face and wash your hands frequently for at least 20 seconds.
  • Please wear protective gear (masks, gloves, etc.) as needed.

As a volunteer you will be expected to coordinate the payment transaction directly with the community member. Community members may or may not have access to Paypal or Venmo, so be prepared to figure out payment with them.

We make all volunteers affirmatively guarantee that they:
  1. Are not exhibiting any symptoms of COVID-19.
  2. Have not, in the past 14 days, participated in gatherings with more than 10 people or traveled through heavily populated areas (ie. airports, train stations, public transit centers, etc).
